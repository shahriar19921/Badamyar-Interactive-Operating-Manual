CHAPTER 19 UTILITIES – MATERIAL HANDLING

SECTION 1 INTRODUCTION

1.1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

1.1.1 Purpose

The objective of the WP4 material handling system is to cover all possible handling required and to ensure the good execution of the following operations:

  • handling during normal operation (equipment, consumables, lubricants, chemicals),
  • routine maintenance during normal periodic visit
  • planned preventive maintenance operations,
  • periodic visits and inspections of equipment and parts,
  • servicing, maintenance and major overhaul of equipment and components.
The handling principle and definition of local handling device depend on considerations such as dimensions and weight of load, type and frequency handling operation and equipment layout and transfer loads.

1.1.2 System Criticality

The handling system is particularly critical for the day-to-day operation (maintenance, chemical…). The more critical the process equipment and the more frequent the maintenance operations on them are, the more critical the corresponding lifting equipment is.

2.1. PEDESTAL CRANE

2.1.1 Overview

Pedestal Crane is considered for the following duties;

  • Access to all laydown areas designated for use by the crane.
  • Capable of reaching and handling all loads identified as requiring maintenance / replacement, directly.
  • Relocation of material handling/maintenance equipment around the platform or from one deck level to another via the laydown areas.
  • Removal of temporary equipment
  • Handling of equipment and materials from/to supply boat and drill barge by crane
  • Personnel Lifting

2.2. PERMANENT LIFTING EQUIPMENT

2.2.1 Monorails

The provision of monorail has been kept to a minimum in quantity and location. The monorails are provided for heavier valves i.e. ESV and 16” Ball valves and some items which required horizontal movement during lifting and the use of pad eyes would be inappropriate.

2.2.2 Pad eye

Pad eyes are provided for pressure safety valves and pulling roller platform. Pad eye for pulling helps roller platform to be moved from equipment location to laydown area.

2.3. MISCELLANEOUS HANDLING EQUIPMENT

2.3.1 Super Beam Clamp

This super beam clamp will be used at unspecified areas without fixed lifting equipment as lifting points, by attaching to the structural steel members. Typical locations are under the deck where direct crane access is not possible. In addition, super beam clamp can be used up to 90 degree in swing direction.

2.3.2 Manual Chain Hoist

Manual chain hoist is used for the majority of lifting applications not directly accessible by platform crane. The hoist shall be spark proof and suitable for use in a hazardous area.

2.3.3 Manual Trolley Hoist

Manual trolley hoist is used for horizontal moving by attaching with monorail. The manual trolley hoist shall be spark proof and suitable for use in a hazardous area.

2.3.4 Cylinder Trolley

Cylinder trolley is used for transporting cylinders respectively around the deck areas i.e. CO2 cylinder. Cylinder trolley shall be supplied with rubber tires suitable for moving loads on grating surfaces.

2.3.5 Platform Truck & Elevating Cart

Platform truck and elevating cart are used to transport individual equipment items, valves and components around the deck areas.

The transportation route is around the perimeter on all decks. The platform truck dimensions shall be suitable for movement on the transportation route.

Platform truck shall be supplied with MC Nylon suitable for moving loads on plating surfaces by using one human pull or push strength.

Elevating cart is used where monorail or pad eye is not feasible due to space constraint.

2.3.6 Roller Platform

Roller Platform is for transferring large bore valves and heavy equipment components. This can be moved by pulling and pushing equipment such as Pusher and Come along with pulling post to Laydown Area.

2.3.7 A-Frame

A-frame is used for lifting when there is no steel structure or not enough space to support other lifting tools such as manual chain hoist and beam clamp or monorail.

2.3.8 Come Along

Come along is a hand tool consisting of a ratchet lever, cable and pulling pad eye used for moving heavy loads by hand or for tightening wire

2.3.9 Shackle, strap and sling

Shackle, strap and sling are used with manual chain hoist and lever hoist for the lifting and pulling applications. The shackle will be used to connect with the pad eye for the installation of the chain hoist or sling. The strap and sling are also used for lifting equipment/part have no lifting lug.

2.3.10 Installation device for Trolley hoist

Installation device for trolley hoist is provided at some locations where is not proper for lifting and installing trolley hoist onto monorails. This device weight is below 10kg, therefore, it can be handled manually.

2.3.11 Portable Platform

Portable platform is provided to access manholes horizontal vessels. Removable handrails are applicable to access easily. 4 Wheels is swivel type to be moved in narrow space.

2.3.12 Elephant Crane

In case of that Super beam clamp or A-frame cannot be used due to the restricted space, Elephant Crane will be used for those handling items such as life rafts below 200kg according to capacity of this crane.
