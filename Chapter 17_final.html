CHAPTER 17 UTILITIES – FRESH WATER

SECTION 1 INTRODUCTION

1.1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

1.1.1 Purpose

Fresh water on WP4 is provided to supply water for a safety shower in the chemical area and a toilet in the Emergency Shelter. The Water Tank ( T-0041 ) is located on the weather deck and gravitationally supplies water to users. The Water Tank (T-0041) is filled by water from a supply boat via a connection at surfer landing. Overflow is provided in the tank to limit the maximum liquid level.

Hide image

1.1.2 System Criticality

The availability of the fresh water system is essential for safety (safety showers / eyebath unit) and therefore particular care should be taken to ensure that the Water Tank T-0041 is full at all time

1.1.3 Key components

The system comprises the following main pieces of equipment:

  • Safety Shower

2.1.1 Water Tank T-0041

Hide image

The purpose of Water Tank (T-0041) is to supply water for the safety shower, the Eye Bath Station and the toilet of the Emergency Shelter. It operates at atmospheric pressure and ambient temperature. The size of the Water Tank (T-0041) was selected to meet the amount of water required for the safety shower and the toilet. The nozzle O1 for the safety shower is located lower than the nozzle O2 of the toilet to ensure sufficient water volume for the safety shower. The elevation difference of the two nozzles was decided for volume between the two nozzles to meet water usage of the safety shower as per ANSI/ISEA code.

SECTION 3 INSTRUMENTATION, CONTROL AND SHUTDOWN

3.1. CONTROL DESCRIPTION

3.1.1 Water Tank Level Control

A level gauge ( LG-50043 ) is provided for the water level control. There will be two operators (one in front of the Water Tank (T-0041) and the other on the surfer landing). They will have walkie-talkies to communicate.

Hide image

The operator in front of the Water Tank (T-0041) looks to LG-50043 and the other handles a pump on the surfer landing. When the water level becomes appropriate, they communicate with walkie-talkies and the pump is stopped.
