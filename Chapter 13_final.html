CHAPTER 13 EXTRACTION WELLS & FLOWLINES – WET GAS METER

SECTION 1 INTRODUCTION

1.1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

1.1.1 Purpose

The Wet Gas Meter measures the water content in the gas and individual flow rate of hydrocarbons and water. The Roxar Wet Gas Meter is designed for installation in wells with gas volume fraction (GVF) > 95 %vol.

1.1.2 System Criticality

This system is not critical since it could be replaced by other analytic methods in case of breakdown. However the time to obtain same accurate analytic result by other way will certainly jeopardize the proper operational reservoir follow-up or decision to offload a well.

1.1.3 Key components



In addition to the meter spool there is electronics enclose which includes power supply and flow computer. Hence the meter can be divided in to three parts: meter body, electronic enclosure and software.

2.1. SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

See Drawing
“Wellhead Platform – WP4-Wet Gas Meter” - MM-YAD-91-HHIE-150106 - Rev 05

Hide image

The meter measures the wet gas flow using 4 individual “raw” measurements: pressure and temperature which is essential to the wet gas properties, differential pressure over a cone to measure flow and microwaves to measure water. In addition hydrocarbon calculation based on equations of state and PVT input gives densities and GOR. Based on this, the meter calculates individual flow rates of gas, condensate, and water.

Information’s are sent to PCS and can be retrieved from QP2-CR or from WP4 Instrument Shelter HMI.

SECTION 3 INSTRUMENTATION, CONTROL AND SHUTDOWN

3.1. CONTROL DESCRIPTION

3.1.1 Test

Before starting the test of one well, operator shall choose the duration of the test, with one timer on test separator graphic view. To start the test, Operator shall press the soft push button on the HMI (or QP2-CR DCS) which is

  • “START WELL 1 TEST”, START WELL 2 TEST”, etc.
Upon activation of this command, close command are given to production manifold switching valve and after some delay open command is sent to the test manifold switching valve.
The purpose is to avoid valve production and test valve can be closed or opened simultaneously.

When Production valve is closed and test valve is opened, test duration timer are starting, and when well test is finished test timer duration is stopped.

Wet gas meter provides only flowrate (gas, condensate, water). Totalizer of flowrate is indicated in PCS display.

When operator change the well number to be tested, currently displayed test separator data are transferred to another display called “LAST TEST RESULT”.

SECTION 4 OPERATING MODES

4.1. OPERATING MODES

Well test operation can be remotely controlled from the QP2 Control Room or from the WP4 HMI on the PCS cabinet of the Instrument Shelter.

4.2. NORMAL START-UP

Start of a Test is done through the DCS using the dedicated interface either from QP2-CR or from HMI.


4.3. NORMAL OPERATIONS

The normal well test operations are performed from the QP2-CR except if the differential pressure alarm PDAH-50001 is activated, indicating a permanent depressurization of the test manifold, which has to be investigated (leak not detectable by the gas detectors, drain valve to Closed Drain system not fully closed or passing)
